Future City

Speculative cartographies of urban infrastructures

Future City is an experimental mapping model that explores future infrastructures of the city. It visualizes interactions across five domains. energy, mobility, data governance, security, and ecology.

Nodes represent system components and agents, and links indicate flows, dependencies, and information exchanges. The map serves as a speculative tool for understanding how urban systems may interoperate beyond their present boundaries.
